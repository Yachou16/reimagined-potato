Burger

This delicious burger with secret ingredients from the ancient world will take you to another dimension. The taste is something you have never ever tasted before. The toppings are fresh vegetables and cheese. The meat comes straight out of the mountains of France Oui Oui!

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 20mins

Servings: 4

  • 1 Large Egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs
